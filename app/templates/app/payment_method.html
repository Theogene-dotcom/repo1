Payment Methods

At Isoko.rw, we offer a variety of secure and convenient payment options to make your shopping experience as smooth as possible. Below are the payment methods we accept:

Credit and Debit Cards

We accept major credit and debit cards, including:

  • Visa
  • MasterCard
  • American Express
  • Discover

All card payments are processed through our secure payment gateway, ensuring your card details are safe and protected.

Mobile Money

For your convenience, we accept payments via mobile money services:

  • MTN Mobile Money
  • Airtel Money

Mobile money payments are quick, secure, and can be done from the comfort of your home.

Bank Transfers

We also accept direct bank transfers. You can transfer the payment directly to our bank account. Our bank details are provided at checkout.

Once the transfer is complete, please email your payment receipt to payments@isoko.rw to confirm your payment.

PayPal

For international customers, we accept payments via PayPal. Pay securely with your PayPal account or credit card through the PayPal gateway.

PayPal payments are instant and secure, ensuring a hassle-free checkout process.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

We offer Cash on Delivery for selected locations. You can pay in cash when your order is delivered to your doorstep.

Please note that COD is available for specific regions and order values. Check eligibility at checkout.

Security

Your payment security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways to process all transactions. Your payment information is confidential and not shared with any third parties.
