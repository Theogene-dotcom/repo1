Returns

At Isoko.rw, we strive to ensure that our customers are completely satisfied with their purchases. If you are not satisfied with your order, you can return it to us for a refund or exchange. Below are the details of our returns policy:

Return Policy

Items can be returned within 30 days of the delivery date. To be eligible for a return, items must be unused, in their original condition, and in the original packaging. Certain types of items cannot be returned, such as perishable goods, custom products, and personal care items.

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@isoko.rw or call +250 788 555 934 to initiate the return process.
  2. Provide your order number and details about the product you wish to return.
  3. Our customer service team will provide you with a return authorization number and instructions on how to return your item.
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and documentation.
  5. Ship the item to the address provided by our customer service team.

Please note that you will be responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the item was damaged or defective.

Processing Your Return

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it to ensure it meets the eligibility criteria. If the return is approved, we will process your refund or exchange within 7-10 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method, and exchanges will be shipped out promptly.

Exceptions

There are some items that cannot be returned, including:

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Personalized items
  • Items marked as final sale

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our returns policy or need assistance with your return, please contact our customer support team:

  • Phone: +250 788 555 934
  • Email: support@isoko.rw
