Money-back Guarantee

At Isoko.rw, we are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality products and services. Our Money-back Guarantee ensures that you can shop with confidence, knowing that if you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund.

Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging. Returns must be made within 30 days of the purchase date.

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@isoko.rw or call +250 788 555 934 to initiate the return process.
  2. Provide your order number and details about the product you wish to return.
  3. Our customer service team will provide you with a return authorization number and instructions on how to return your item.
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and documentation.
  5. Ship the item to the address provided by our customer service team.

Please note that you will be responsible for the cost of return shipping.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it to ensure it meets the eligibility criteria. If the return is approved, we will process your refund within 7-10 business days. The refund will be applied to your original method of payment. You will receive a confirmation email once your refund has been processed.

Exceptions

The following items are not eligible for a money-back guarantee:

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Personalized items
  • Items marked as final sale

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Money-back Guarantee or need assistance with your return, please contact our customer support team:

  • Phone: +250 788 555 934
  • Email: support@isoko.rw
